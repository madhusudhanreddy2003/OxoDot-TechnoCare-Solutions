About Us

OxoDot TechnoCare Solutions is an IT company dedicated to providing technology-driven solutions for hospitals, health systems, and medical groups. It specializes in offering end-to-end RCM services, physician advisory services, practice management, revenue integrity solutions, medical coding management, EHR/EMR technology services, business office services, quality management programs, business intelligence and analysis and more. Additionally, the company provides business process outsourcing and information technology services to local Government Departments and NGOs within India. Also to few Clients of US, UK, MiddleEast. OxoDot also offers a wide range of services, including CFO advisory, environmental and social governance services, financial planning and analysis, enterprise risk and compliance services, human capital management, catering to various industries to healthcare, aviation, automotive, public services, retail, technology, AI-ML and agriculture sercives/ development/ RND.
